UMN Syllabus Maker

Fill out the form to receive a list of all the dates when your course will meet during the specified semester. Alternatively, you can download a syllabus template (in Word, LaTeX, or HTML formats) prepopulated with a class schedule and other required information. This application uses the academic calendars posted Onestop Student Service to determine when classes will be cancelled for holidays or University recesses.

This is just an experiment at the moment. If you find this useful, or you'd like to see a more full-featured syllabus management tool, let us know.
